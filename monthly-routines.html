Last Friday of the Month (Evening)

  • Gather with colleagues or teammates to celebrate achievements and express gratitude for their contributions.
  • Reflect on team performance and identify areas for growth or improvement.
  • Plan team-building activities or initiatives to strengthen collaboration and morale.

Last Sunday of the Month

  • Conduct monthly health check-ups, including physical exams and screenings.
  • Review financial goals and spending habits, adjusting budgets or investments as needed.
  • Reflect on personal and professional achievements over the past month, celebrating successes and identifying areas for growth.
  • Set specific goals and intentions for the upcoming month, breaking them down into actionable steps.

First Sunday of the Month

  • Follow the morning routine as usual.
  • Dedicate extra time to reflection and goal-setting for the month ahead.
  • Review long-term goals and milestones, adjusting plans and priorities as necessary.
  • Create a monthly calendar or schedule to track important dates and deadlines.
