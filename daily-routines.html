Everyday Morning

Start your day right with these morning rituals:

  • Wake up at 4 am
  • Drink a glass of water and perform personal hygiene
  • Exercise with warm-up, Surya Namaskar, and 30 minutes of yoga
  • Practice Pranayama for 15 minutes, followed by a 15-minute meditation session
  • Write your goals and plan for the day

Everyday Afternoon

Stay energized throughout the day with these afternoon routines:

  • Eat a balanced lunch before 1:30 pm
  • Take a 10-minute break every hour for a short walk

Everyday Evening

Wind down and prepare for a restful night with these evening rituals:

  • Do 20 minutes of yoga and 10 minutes of meditation
  • Have a light dinner before 7:00 pm
  • Review tasks identified for the day and prioritize tasks for the evening
  • Dedicate time to skill development through online courses, reading, or practice
  • Practice gratitude and spend time outdoors connecting with nature
  • Engage in self-care activities
  • Spend quality time with family or friends
  • Engage in a creative project or brainstorm innovative ideas
  • Practice mindful communication with others
  • Volunteer for a cause aligned with your interests and goals

Everyday Before Sleep

End your day on a peaceful note with these bedtime routines:

  • Write down thoughts on paper to clear your mind
  • Reflect on planned and unplanned tasks
  • Write down goals and tasks for the next day
  • Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing
  • Wash eyes with cold water to reduce strain
  • Create a calming bedtime routine

Any time of the day

These practices can be incorporated into any part of your day to maintain focus and productivity:

  • Write down thoughts on paper to clear your mind
  • Practice deep breathing exercises for relaxation and focus
  • Ensure mindfulness in every activity, focusing on the present moment
  • Take a 5-minute break after deep focus sessions
  • Plan work tasks in 15-minute intervals using the timer method

Any time of the day - planning the unplanned

Be prepared for unplanned tasks and emergencies with these strategies:

  • Write down unplanned tasks and plan them for the next day
  • For urgent tasks, give them 1 minute of thought and decide whether to do, skip, delegate, or delay

Any time of the day - avoid these

Avoid these habits to maintain a healthy and productive lifestyle:

  • No snacking between meals
  • Avoid watching TV or any other entertainment during work time
  • Avoid sugary and salty drinks and snacks; opt for green tea or herbal tea
  • Avoid consuming chips, cookies, or fryums as snacks; opt for fruits or nuts
  • Avoid multitasking; focus on one task at a time
